What is coating?

Foam-coated furniture refers to innovative furniture made from high-density polyurethane foam coated with a special finish to enhance durability and design flexibility.

Key Features of Foam-Coated Furniture

Lightweight yet Sturdy High-density polyurethane foam offers a lightweight structure while remaining durable. The special coating applied on the surface adds strength and makes it suitable for long-term use.
Duravle and Waterproof The coating provides protection against UV rays, water, and weather conditions, making foam-coated furniture ideal for both indoor and outdoor use.
Limitless Design Possibilites Thanks to the flexibility of foam as a material, designers can easily create intricate curves and unique shapes. Moreover, the coating comes in various colors and textures, allowing for custom designs that perfectyl match any space.
Easy Miantenance The coating can be re-applied when damaged, and some brands even oer free recoating services. This help extend the furniture's lifespan.

Why is Foam-Coated Furniture Revolutionary?

Breaks the Limitations of Traditional Materials Unlike wood or metal, foam is easier to mold into complex shapes and curves, enabling the realization of bold and creative ideas that were previously hard to achieve.
Versatile Applications It works well in hotel lobbies, cafes, commercial spaces, and residential areas. Its unique design adds a distinctive character to any environment.
Eco-Friendly Potential Some brands adopt sustainable materials and processes during anufacturing. Additionally, the long-lasting durability of foam-coated furniture reduces waste, making it an environmentally conscious choice.

How does it work?

01. Design input

Enjoy the liberty to draw your own custom design. This design must be converted in to a STEP, DXF or DWG file. Exported in the CNC cutting machine of the foam cutter

02. Foam cutting

Polyurethane foam cutting with a CNC mahine typically involves programming a CAD model of the disered shape into the machine's software. The foam material is then secured onto the machine's worktable and the cutting tool is controlled by the software to precisely cut or carve the foam into the desired shape. The resulting foam piece can then be further processed or used as a finished product depending on the intended application.

03. Coating

1. Preparation
Ensure the foam is clean and free of any debris before starting the coating process.
2. Coating steps
First Layer (Primer) - Acts as a base layer that provides dhesion to the foam. - Fills in small holes on the foam surface to create a smooth foundation.
Second Layer (PU Layer) - A high-quality polyurethane layer that reinforces the coating. - Adds strength and durability to the surface.
Third Layer (Topcoat) - A soft-touch color layer that provides the final finish. - Enhances the aesthetic appeal and gives the desired texture.
3. Curing
- Allow the coating to cure overnight. - The product is ready for shipping the following day.
